Alliance of 6 Nations

The Alliance of 6 Nations is a recent strategic alliance of the nations on the Elathian continent who oppose the expansionistic tendencies of the Malinval Orcs, as well as the worship of Nera, Mat'raktha, and Malik. Made up of Curinor, Eldoran, Demal Thor, Kandor, Gerasalim, and Dormania, the alliance was impossible until recently because of recurring wars between the elves and dwarves over the Mithril Hills. The crowning achievement of the Alliance has been the construction, maintenance, and manning of the Fortress of Light and other border forts along the Blighted Gap.
